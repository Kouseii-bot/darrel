Our Services

At Iron Axe Guitars, we offer a range of services to meet all of your musical needs. Whether you're a seasoned musician or a beginner, our expert team is here to help you unleash the power of your sound.

  • Custom Guitar Design: Get a guitar tailored to your unique style and preferences.
  • Repairs and Restorations: From minor fixes to major overhauls, we bring old guitars back to life.
  • Metal Gear Accessories: Enhance your guitar experience with custom straps, picks, and more.
  • Private Lessons: Learn to master your instrument with one-on-one lessons tailored to your needs.
